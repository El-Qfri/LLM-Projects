# Monthly Mood Tracker

Position a circular visualization beside the mood slider.

The tracker consists of **four concentric circles** representing the days of the month.

* Center circle → Day 1
* Second ring → Days 2–11
* Third ring → Days 12–21
* Fourth ring → Days 22–31

This creates a total of **31 mood segments**.

Whenever the user records their mood, the corresponding day's segment is filled with the selected mood color.

Previously recorded days remain colored, allowing the tracker to become a colorful emotional map of the month.

# Vintage Radio

Place a beautifully illustrated **vintage wooden radio** in the center of the page.

The radio should have:

* Wooden body
* Metallic knobs
* Retro aesthetic inspired by 1950s–1960s radios

When the user selects a mood, the radio automatically recommends and plays music that matches the mood.

Examples:

**Joyful**
→ Happy Playlist

**Sad**
→ Healing Playlist

**Calm**
→ Lo-fi, Nature Sounds, Jazz

The music can be powered by either:

* Spotify API
* YouTube Music

Next to the radio, include a simple music player with:

* Play/Pause
* Skip
* Volume Control
* Progress Bar

# Mood Cat 🐱

Below the radio, display a cute cartoon cat as the app's emotional companion.

Next to the cat, place a speech bubble containing a randomly selected motivational quote.

Example quotes:

> Every storm runs out of rain.

> Small steps still move you forward.

A new quote should appear every day.

---

# Daily Tiny Story

Below the cat, display a notebook-style card.

Each day, present a very short inspirational story (approximately 3–5 lines).

Example:

> A tiny seed spent the entire winter hidden beneath the soil. No one noticed it. But when spring arrived, it became the first flower in the garden.

A different story should be selected randomly each day.

# Future Features (Version 2)

## 🌸 AI Mood Insights

Use AI to analyze mood history and identify emotional patterns.

For example:

* "You tend to feel calmer on Tuesdays."
* "Your mood usually improves after listening to relaxing music."
* "This month shows more positive emotions than last month."

---

## 🌺 Bloom Garden

Every day the user logs their mood, a flower grows in their personal virtual garden.

Over weeks and months, the garden becomes a beautiful visual representation of the user's emotional journey.

This feature encourages daily engagement while perfectly matching the app's name, **MoodBloom**.

---

## Design Philosophy

MoodBloom should feel more than just a mood tracker—it should be a warm, calming companion that helps users understand, reflect on, and gently improve their emotional well-being. The interface should be colorful, cozy, minimalist, and emotionally uplifting, creating a sense of peace every time the app is opened.
